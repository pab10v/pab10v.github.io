Core Technologies

SNI Fragmentation

Transparently splits TLS ClientHello packets into variable-sized fragments, defeating SNI-based filtering and fingerprinting.

Polymorphic Engine

Powered by Go & WebAssembly. Uses Markov Chains to dynamically evolve evasion tactics (Split, Disorder, Chaff) based on network conditions.

Mesh Intelligence

A decentralized P2P mesh network that shares real-time DPI detection telemetry between instances to adapt globally.

Fail-Open Safety

Advanced PAC script logic ensures you never lose connection. If the obfuscation layer fails, Bye falls back to direct traffic instantly.

Filtering Policy

Bye is a Neutral Transport Layer. We do not inspect, modify, or filter the content of your communications. Our policy is strictly defined by:

  • No Content Inspection: We only modify the transport-layer "shape" of packets.
  • Zero Logging: No URLs, IPs, or payloads are ever persisted.
  • Bypass by Default: Critical infrastructure, local networks, and banking domains are automatically bypassed to ensure security and compliance.

Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

We believe in absolute privacy. Bye does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All obfuscation happens locally on your machine. Peer-to-peer telemetry shared via the mesh is strictly limited to network performance metrics and contains no identifying information.
